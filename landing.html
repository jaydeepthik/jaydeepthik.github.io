About Me

Get to know me better!.

I am an incoming Research Scientist at Toyota Research Institute - North America (TRI-NA)
I completed my Masters degree in Computer Science and Engineering from The Ohio State University in Spring 2021.
My interests revolve around machine learning, deep learning, reinforcement learning and data science as a whole.
I like to experiment with different learning algorithms and convert them into real life applications.

I am on a mission to make machines learn, think, understand and imaging one gradient descent step at a time !

Who am I?

I think that is the most philosophical question that anyone can (or rather should) ask themselves. Oof this turned into a philosophical lesson !!
This is something that I like doing a lot. I like thinking about different situations and observe peoples opinions on them.
I am a funny guy, who can crack jokes (often bad) on the drop of a hat.

I think I have found my passion early in life. My passion revolves around Deep Learning, Machine Learning, Reinforcement learning, Algorithms, Physics, Mathematics and Statistics. I like building things using my knowledge and skills.I like questioning and understanding things happening under the hood of these learning algorithms.

Apart from this, I love playing football (Soccer) a lot. If I have played football with you, I will probably remember you my entire life. Apart from football, I have played badminton, snooker, volleyball, tennis, cricket and hockey for a fairly long time.
I like doodling, sketching, photography, biking and reading books in my free time.

If you feel like sharing your thoughts about any of the topics that I mentioned above, feel free to drop a message and lets talk over a cup of coffee / tea (PS. I am a tea person).
